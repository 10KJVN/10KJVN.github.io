Development

I was asked to look for possibilities to create custom overrides for unity's volumes. So i went on ahead and found out it's pretty simple which led to the creation of 2 scripts.

A 'TintRendererFeature' and a 'CustomPostScreenTint'. These two scripts work together to apply the tint to your screen.
