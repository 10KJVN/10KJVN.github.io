Development

I was asked to look for possibilities to create custom overrides for unity's volumes. So i went on ahead and found out it's pretty simple and created 3 scripts.

A CustomRenderPassFeature, TintRendererFeature & CustomPostScreenTint. Each of these serve a purpose to make the effect render (show up).
